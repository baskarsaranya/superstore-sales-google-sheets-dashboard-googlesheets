
# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard
## Google Sheets Interactive Dashboard

## Overview
End-to-end sales performance dashboard analyzing 10,000+ orders 
from a fictional retail superstore across 4 years (2021-2024). 
Built entirely in Google Sheets using pivot tables, QUERY formulas, 
and data visualization.

## Key Business Insights
1. High discounts (>40%) generate -108% profit margin — the 
   business loses more than $1 for every $1 of revenue at high 
   discount levels
2. Tables and Bookcases are loss-making sub-categories despite 
   strong sales volumes
3. California alone accounts for 20% of total revenue
4. 2024 shows strongest growth — consistent upward trend across 
   all months vs prior years
5. Standard Class shipping carries 60% of all orders but has the 
   lowest profit margin at 11.3%
6. No-discount orders generate 34% profit margin vs -108% for 
   high-discount orders
